Features

Layer sounds naturally

Place sounds into three slots and turn environmental recordings, drones, and textures into one calm atmosphere.

Slowly evolving texture

UTP does more than loop a file. It blends small fragments over time to create a spacious sound that keeps moving gently.

Smooth transitions

Playback, stopping, skipping, and swapping sounds are eased with gentle fades, so quiet moments are not interrupted.

Balanced loudness

The app is designed to account for differences between source materials, helping layered sounds stay comfortable.

Soft final polish

Layered sounds are shaped to reduce sharp peaks and keep the result calm enough for sleep, focus, or meditation.

Sounds fit together

Pitch and tone can be adjusted so different materials feel more natural when they are played together.

Plays in the background

Use it on iPhone, iPad, and macOS, and control playback from the system media controls.

One-time asset unlocks

Start with the included free assets, then add only the sound packs you want with one-time purchases.
